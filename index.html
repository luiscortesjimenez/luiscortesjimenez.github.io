About Me

Welcome! I am a junior year Computer Science student at Sonoma State University. My goal is to complete my undergraduate degree and then pursue graduate studies where I can apply my interest in computers to my love for nature. Eventually, I hope to help develop and improve Computer Vision models for the identification and protection of endangered plant species using high resolution satellite imagery.

Hobbies & Interests

  • Playing Guitar: I love playing the guitar. My favorite band is The Red Hot Chili Peppers. I also really like playing John Mayer's live stuff. Recently, I built Chord Architect, which is a C++ tool that generates and visualizes guitar chord voicings across the fretboard.
  • Hiking: I enjoy hiking and exploring the outdoors. My favorite local hike is the Mt Tamalpais in Marin. I also love the hike to Alamere Falls! Taylor Mountain and Cobblestone Trail are also great options.
  • Plant Conservation: I have a small green house in my backyard where I grow and preserve rare and endangered cacti and succulents. Check out my Cacti / Succulents page for photos.
